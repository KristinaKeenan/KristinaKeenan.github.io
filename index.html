Who exactly is...

Kristina Keenan?





I'm a Rochester-born junior at Ithaca College majoring in Emerging Media (B.S.) with a concentration in Media Design and Production. I love media today in all of its various forms- games, film, television, web media- the list goes on and on. Right now I'm looking to make my way into the world of game development! Take a look at my site to see a few of the things I've been working on, or drop me an email at kkeenan@ithaca.edu!

Majoring in...

Emerging Media?

Game Development

I'm extremely interested in game design and development, and have gotten to experiment within the medium in a variety of ways:

-Working with both the Unreal and Unity game engines

-Working with the Stencyl game making application and Phaser, a JavaScript game engine API

-Attended GDC 2016 as a Microsoft Women in Gaming "Game Changer"

-Vice president of the Ithaca College Game Developers Club

Media Production

From consuming to producing it, visual media is a field that I've always loved. Here are some examples of how I've become involved with its production:

-Recording audio using in studio using a sound board and mixer, recording and booming on set using various Tascam recorders

-Editing and mixing audio using ProTools and Adobe Audition

-Filming and editing video using Avid and Adobe Premiere

-Produced and coordinated student film

Computer Science

A huge part of what drives my interests in media and game development is a love of computers and computing technologies, which includes the following oppurtunities:

-Worked with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, JQuery, and HTML5 Canvas

-Worked with Python, Java, C#, C++

-Scholarship Coordinator for Ithaca College Women in Computing

Other?

Additionally I've gotten the chance to produce media in a variety of other mediums, including content for the Ithaca College Television show that I currently produce, Game Over:

-Writing segments and producing corresponding images using Adobe PhotoShop for Game Over

-Running teleprompter, floor managing, and social media managing for Game Over

-Modeling, animating, and writing Python programs to generate 3D models and graphics with Autodesk Maya
