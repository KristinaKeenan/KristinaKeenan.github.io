About

Hi! I'm a recent graduate of Ithaca College who was born and raised in Rochester, NY. At school I majored in Emerging Media and minored in Game Development. I'm looking to create interactive designs, strengthen my project management skills, and continue to develop immersive media.

I've dabbled in many forms of media production, from producing ICTV's longest running show, Game Over, to producing, designing, and programming my senior thesis, Infinity Lab, a virtual reality puzzle game. Away from work, I'm a lover of pop culture and listen to way too many podcasts.
